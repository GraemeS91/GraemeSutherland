About Me

“I was 23 when my mum was diagnosed with early‑onset Alzheimer’s. It changed everything.” I’m Graeme Sutherland, a full‑time carer and Alzheimer’s advocate from Glasgow. When my mum was diagnosed at 59, I stepped into the role of her primary carer. In 2021, I chose to share our daily life online—not just the difficult moments, but the laughter, joy, and deep connection that remain. I want people to see that there is still life beyond a diagnosis. Since then, my content has touched millions. I’ve used personal storytelling to educate, reduce stigma, and build a global community of carers and families. Along the way, I’ve become an Alzheimer Scotland ambassador and shared our journey on platforms like ITV’s *This Morning*, STV News, Al Jazeera, and BBC Scotland.
